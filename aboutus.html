After almost four years in Newcastle Australia, ministering to 12-17 year olds from unchurched backgrounds, through discipleship, music, worship, project planning and program coordination, we became students in YWAM Tijuana's Children at Risk School.

Through this training we were equipped to not only minister effectively to children from a wide range of "at risk" situations, but learned a little more how to be the people and parents God created us to be!

We now live and minister in Nashville, Tennessee, beginning our own family and having our own children. Shawn is Youth Ministries Director at Hillsboro Presbyterian Church.
