Five Day Extraveganza!

We wanted to update you all (and BIG thanks those of you who gave financially towards our fees) on our annual 5 Day Escape (5DE) camp that we ran from October 5-9. We loaded 50 (mostly unchurched) kids and 30 staff into a double decker coach bus and drove 10 hours north to the Gold Coast. Shawn led (and drove) a team that went up a day early to set up sound gear and prepare the awesome games and activities. Sarah was Camp Coordinator and was the "hands-on" "go-to" girl for the whole camp and Shawn was sound and media tech and band liason and leader for worship and music. It was a fantastic week full of games, outdoor laser tag, canoeing, water tubing, going to an amusement park (Dream World!), having live bands every night, and an awesome Gospel message and response opportunity on the final night. 20+ kids responded for the first time, either making commitments to follow the Lord or wanting to know more about Him. It was definitely 5 Days of Exhaustion for us, but worth every bit of it! Alot of the kids that made decisions have begun coming along to our weekly youth program, Youth Street where we offer consistent discipleship, encouragement, inclusiveness, dynamic program, honour and developement in activities like surf, sport, skate, music art and dance. So... stay tuned for more on the exciting lives of the Cothrans!
